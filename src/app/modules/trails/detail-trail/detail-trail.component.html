Shiba Inu Dog Breed

The Shiba Inu is the smallest of the six original and distinct spitz breeds of dog from Japan. A small, agile dog that copes very well with mountainous terrain, the Shiba Inu was originally bred for hunting.

Head out on this 1.3-km loop trail near Marecos, Porto District. Generally considered an easy route, it takes an average of 19 min to complete. This trail is great for mountain biking, running, and walking, and it's unlikely you'll encounter many other people while exploring. The trail is open year-round and is beautiful to visit anytime. Dogs are welcome and may be off-leash in some areas.
